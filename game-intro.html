The Timeline

49 BC - Ancient Egypt

Assassin's Creed Origins

Witness the birth of the Brotherhood during the reign of Cleopatra. The era of pharaohs is ending, and a new order is rising.

1789 AD - French Revolution

Assassin's Creed Unity

Paris is burning. Amidst the chaos of the Revolution, the Assassins fight to expose the true powers behind the guillotine.

1868 AD - Industrial Revolution

Assassin's Creed Syndicate

London is entering the modern age. The invention of electricity and trains changes the world, and the way Assassins fight.

The Assassin's Arsenal

Weapons evolve with history. Adaptation is key to survival.

The Medjay's Shield

AC: Origins

In ancient times, defense was as crucial as offense. Bayek utilizes a shield to block arrows and bash enemies, a lost art in modern assassinations.

The Phantom Blade

AC: Unity

A modification of the Hidden Blade that fires a silent projectile. It allows Arno to assassinate from a distance without raising an alarm.

The Rope Launcher

AC: Syndicate

London's streets are too wide for parkour. The Gauntlet is fitted with a rope launcher, allowing rapid ascent to rooftops and zip-lining across avenues.
